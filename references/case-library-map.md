## Dominant Lessons

High-frequency lessons in the source corpus:

- Narrow workflow beats broad platform.
- The closer a tool is to revenue, conversion, or cost reduction, the stronger willingness to pay.
- Professional repeated workflows are strong productization candidates.
- Small tools can monetize when embedded in a daily workflow.
- AI is valuable when it sells usable results, not AI as a concept.
- Failure usually appears in broken economics, weak differentiation, poor product delivery, platform dependence, or trust/governance gaps.

## Retrieval Guidance

Search by workflow words, not only industry words.

Examples:

- For "教培老师 AI 工具", also search `培训`, `课程`, `在线课程`, `评估`, `报告`, `内容产品`.
- For "销售跟进", also search `CRM`, `销售`, `线索`, `转化`, `客户协作`.
- For "本地商家工具", also search `预约`, `报价`, `支付`, `客户提醒`, `表单`.
- For "AI 内容工具", also search `结果`, `转写`, `翻译`, `报告`, `创作`, `workflow`.

Always pair success cases with failure counterexamples.
